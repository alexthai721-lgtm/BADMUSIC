# üéµ BADMUSIC - Your Lyrics, Your Sound, in 24 Hours

BADMUSIC is a modern web application that bridges the gap between lyricists and music producers. It allows aspiring artists to submit their lyrics, choose their desired musical style, and receive a professionally produced song in under 24 hours. The app leverages the power of the Google Gemini API to provide instant creative feedback and generate tailored song ideas, making the production process faster and more collaborative.

## ‚ú® Key Features

- **AI-Powered Song Idea Generation:** Instantly get suggestions for mood, tempo, instrumentation, and chord progressions based on your lyrics.
- **Instant Lyric Analysis:** Get real-time feedback on your lyrics' sentiment and rhyme scheme before submission.
- **Full Creative Control:** Specify any musical genre, choose between a male or female vocalist, and select from various song structures and vocal styles.
- **Global Reach (Multi-Language Support):** A fully internationalized interface with a language selector supporting English, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese, and Italian.
- **Seamless Payments:** An animated pricing carousel with clear options for single-song or lifetime production plans, integrated directly with PayPal.
- **Direct-to-Producer Workflow:** A streamlined process that sends all submission details and AI-generated ideas directly to the producer's email after payment.
- **Modern & Responsive UI:** A sleek, beautiful, and fully responsive design that works flawlessly on any device, featuring smooth animations and video backgrounds.
